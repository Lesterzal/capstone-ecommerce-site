# Capstone-ecommerce-site

My Project ecommerce website sells walnut desk accessories. At The Walnut Hive, our creative process is guided by the values of nature, design, and balance. We are committed to using sustainably sourced, noble, and natural materials of the highest quality in order to ensure that our products are not only beautiful, but also durable and functional. 

We specialize in creating beautifully crafted desk accessories made out of high-quality walnut wood. Our products are designed to elevate your workspace and bring a touch of warmth and elegance to your daily routine.

Somehting uniqe about our company is We create beautiful and functional bee hives that are designed to give back to the environment by supporting the local bee population. We believe that it's our responsibility
to take care of our planet, and we're dedicated to doing our part by creating sustainable products that benefit both our customers and the environment.
